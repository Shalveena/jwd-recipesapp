Indian inspired chickpeas and kale curry

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp sweet paprika powder
  • 4 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cans of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 4 cups of kale
  • 4 Tbsp olive oil

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan on low heat. When the oil is warm, add the onions and fry until the onions are soft.
  2. Add garlic and continue to fry until the garlic starts to get crispy/starts to change colour.
  3. Add the spices: sweet paprika, cumin and turmeric. Cook for just a few seconds, careful not to let it burn!
  4. Add the chickpeas and cook, uncovered for a few minutes to allow the chickpeas and spices to fry and come together.
  5. Add a dash of water and continue to cook the chickpeas, covered, allowing them to absorb the flavours. Check from time to time that the chickpeas are not getting stuck to the pan; if they are, add some more water as needed.
  6. Smash about half of the chickpeas with the back of the spoon. Add the stock, mix well, cover and let it simmer for another couple of minutes.
  7. Add the kale, cover and cook until the kale is cooked to your liking. Mix it from time to time to allow the kale to mix with the rest of the curry and to cook better.
